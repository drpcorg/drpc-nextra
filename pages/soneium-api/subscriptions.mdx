## Subscriptions

Enables real-time event tracking by subscribing to blockchain activities through WebSockets.

[eth_subscribe](/soneium-api/subscriptions/eth_subscribe): Creates a subscription for live blockchain events. \
[eth_unsubscribe](/soneium-api/subscriptions/eth_unsubscribe): Removes an active subscription to stop receiving updates.
