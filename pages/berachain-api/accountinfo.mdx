## Accounts info

Provides details about an account’s balance, smart contract code, or stored information.

[eth_accounts](/bartio-api/accountinfo/eth_accounts) :  Enumerates all accounts managed by the client. \
[eth_getBalance](/bartio-api/accountinfo/eth_getBalance) : Retrieves the Ether balance of a specified account in Wei. \
[eth_getCode](/bartio-api/accountinfo/eth_getCode) :  Obtains the smart contract bytecode deployed at a particular address. \
[eth_getProof](/bartio-api/accountinfo/eth_getProof) : Generates a Merkle proof for an account’s balance or its storage contents. \
[eth_getStorageAt](/bartio-api/accountinfo/eth_getStorageAt) : Fetches the value stored at a specific storage slot of an account.

These endpoints are essential for managing and interacting with accounts on the Berachain network.
