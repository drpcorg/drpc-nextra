## Gas estimation

Calculates gas fees for transactions and offers both historical and up-to-date gas price insights.

[eth_feeHistory](/bartio-api/gasestimation/eth_feeHistory) : Accesses past gas fee information over a defined block range. \
[eth_estimateGas](/bartio-api/gasestimation/eth_estimateGas) : Projects the gas amount necessary to carry out a specific transaction. \
[eth_gasPrice](/bartio-api/gasestimation/eth_gasPrice) :  Retrieves the prevailing gas price on the Berachain network. \
[eth_createAccessList](/bartio-api/gasestimation/eth_createAccessList) : Constructs an access list to enhance efficiency and minimize gas usage. \
[eth_maxPriorityFeePerGas](/bartio-api/gasestimation/eth_maxPriorityFeePerGas) : Obtains the highest priority fee per gas unit that can be offered.

These endpoints are crucial for optimizing and controlling transaction expenses on the Berachain network.
